Our 6D-Process

01.

Discover.

We seek to understand where you are and where you wish to go, taking the time to grasp your marketing goals and objectives.

02.

Define.

We begin by defining the necessary project requirements and agreeing on specific deliverables. Then, we put the web strategy in place.

03.

Design.

After assessing the goals, specifications, and requirements, we proceed to design the website's architecture, prioritizing usability, searchability, and simplicity through a clean, professional design.

04.

Develop.

In the development stage, the finalized design files are handed off to the development team to implement the code and backend systems, making the website interactive. Before the launch, a beta site is posted for testing and editing to ensure that your expectations and goals are met.

05.

Deploy.

We ensure that your team comprehends the CMS structure, technology, and website functionality. We provide on-site maintenance training to facilitate a smooth transition. The site is monitored for 60 days post-launch to ensure all aspects function properly.

06.

Deliver.

We establish communication links with potential and existing customers using ethical best-practice SEO (Search Engine Optimization) techniques and social media marketing.
